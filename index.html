The Exchange of Ideas

What is a Prediction Market?

A prediction market is a public exchange where people can trade contracts based on the outcomes of future events. Instead of trading stocks, you trade on your beliefs about what will happen in the world.

Price = Probability

Every outcome has shares with prices from $0.01 to $0.99. This price reflects the market's consensus on the probability of an event happening.

  • A share price of $0.72 means a 72% chance.
  • A share price of $0.15 suggests a 15% chance.

When the event resolves, correct shares pay out $1.00.

From Insight to Income: Example Trades

Example 1: The Tech Prediction

You believe Apple is closer to launching a foldable phone than the market thinks.

  • ➤Market: "Will Apple launch a foldable iPhone by 2026?"
  • ➤"Yes" shares trade at $0.31 (31% chance).
  • ➤Your Action: Buy 100 "Yes" shares for $31.
  • ➤Outcome: Apple announces the phone. The event resolves to "Yes".
  • ➤Your Payout: Your shares are now worth $1.00 each, paying out $100.
Your Profit: $69

Example 2: The Economic Forecast

You predict Singapore's economy will grow slower than the market expects.

  • ➤Market: "Will Singapore's GDP growth exceed 3% in 2026?"
  • ➤"No" shares are priced at $0.45 (since "Yes" is $0.55).
  • ➤Your Action: Buy 200 "No" shares for $90.
  • ➤Outcome: GDP grew by 2.8%. The event resolves to "No".
  • ➤Your Payout: Your shares are now worth $1.00 each, paying out $200.
Your Profit: $110
